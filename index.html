Our Values

  • Humility: we respect uncertainty and size positions accordingly.
  • Evidence‑based: we favor data, base rates, and documented processes.
  • Transparency: clear votes, recorded minutes, unitized capital accounts.
  • Stewardship: cost‑aware, tax‑aware execution and simple portfolios.
  • Long‑term: compounding over headline‑chasing.

How We Operate

  • Decisions by member vote per our partnership agreement.
  • Authorized members execute trades on behalf of the partnership.
  • Unitized capital accounting for fair contributions and withdrawals.
  • Assets held at a U.S. brokerage in the partnership’s name (EIN).
