Learning Markets, Together

A student-run investment club exploring public markets through collaborative research and shared decision‑making. Non‑commercial and educational.

Read Research Disclosures

What We Do

  • Run paper and live portfolios owned by the partnership.
  • Study portfolio construction, risk, and behavioral finance.
  • Publish general, not personalized, educational research.

What We Don’t Do

  • No client onboarding or outside-asset management.
  • No individualized investment advice.
  • No compensation for advice or referrals.

How We Operate

We make decisions by member vote per our partnership agreement. Capital accounts are unitized for fairness; contributions and withdrawals are processed at current NAV. Trading is executed by authorized members only.

  • Meetings: Regular sessions to review research and votes.
  • Custody: All assets held at a U.S. brokerage in the partnership’s name.
  • Records: Minutes and member capital ledgers maintained internally.
